Magic Garden

Design Process:

The design process for me was essentially figuring out how to create this game step by step.

I realized early on that utilizing object states were going to be extremely useful, and essentially took it from there!

I created states for each phase of the flower growing process (falling, expanding, growing, blossoming, etc.)

and also created functions that would be called in those specific states (eg. drop, rotate, grow), to display the growth changes.
